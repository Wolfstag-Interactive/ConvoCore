Detailed Description

Drop-on component that implements both IConvoCoreFadeIn and IConvoCoreFadeOut by fading a CanvasGroup or a Renderer material's alpha over a configurable duration.

Resolution order:

  1. A CanvasGroup on this GameObject.
  2. A Renderer on this GameObject or a child.

For Renderer fading the material must expose a _BaseColor (URP) or _Color (Built-In) property and use a blending mode that supports transparency.

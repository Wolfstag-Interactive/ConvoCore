Detailed Description

Scene singleton that tracks all ConvoCoreSpawnPoint instances by their ID.

Spawn points self-register on OnEnable and self-unregister on OnDisable. WorldPointBehaviour queries this registry at conversation begin to resolve named spawn point IDs to world transforms.
